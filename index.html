  • Joseph Hyunjun Lee FOUNDER/ CEO of eKYSS co. ltd.

    As a former employee of Samsung C&T, he founded an educational company in 2001 and has been working in education business for 18 years until now. Currently, he is developing an AI-based global language platform called 'MYPOOL' to be introduced in 2020. His book, My Heart is an Unstoppable Engine<2009>, was selected as the book recommended for CEOs by Korea National Library. He is also a co-author of “Today is the First Day of My Life” and “Era of Practice”, co-written by David Hwang, a Google engineer, who is also known as the founder member of eKYSS and childhood friend of Lee. In 2018, he published “The 2nd Coin War” with David Hwang and it is ranked top 5th bestseller in Kyobo Book Centre’s economics and management area. He developed MPCOIN, the first edutech-based life coin in Korea based on MAPOOL Smart Learning and also has developed MPM which will be applied to 'MYPOOL', a new service for advanced to the global, aiming for the only global language app service for Asians.
